Cranberry Almond Crumble




The "Cranberry Almond Crumble" is a perfect recipe for Fall! It is sweet with a hint of sourness when you taste the cranberries! All it requires is 6 simple ingredients: almonds, cranberries, honey, cocoa powder, oats, and vanilla biscuits! I created this recipe based on what I had at home, since I felt like making something simple but festive. I hope you enjoy this recipe as much as my family and I did! ~Aishani U.




Ingredients:

    2.5 teaspoons of powdered vanilla biscuits

    3/4 teaspoon honey

    3/4 teaspoon cocoa powder

    14 crushed almonds

    Desired amount of dried cranberries

    4-5 teaspoons of instant oats (dry, not cooked)




Instructions:

    1) In a mortar, crush the 14 almonds until it becomes small chunks and transfer the crushed almonds into a seperate bowl.

    2) Throughly mix the vanilla biscuit powder and oats with the almonds.

    3) Add the desired amount of dried cranberries into the mixture and mix thoroughly.

    4) Slowly incorporate the honey into the mixture. Make sure to mix the mixture with your hands to make sure the flavor gets into every grain.

    5) Add the cocoa powder slowly into the mixture and mix well with your hands.

    6) Put the mixture in a seperate bowl (suitable for eating out of) and press it down with a spoon until firm. If needed, press a smaller bowl into the mixture to make it extra firm.

    7) Store in the freezer for a couple of hours (maybe even more).

    8) Take it out the fridge and enjoy! :)
